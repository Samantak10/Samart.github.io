About Me

I am Samantak and a Free-Time Artist

When the pandemic started and we went under lockdown there wasn't much to to do at home. Gradually I realised a lot of people from the comfort of their homes were either into productive stuff or into dalgona coffee. I decided to be a part of the former clan. Hence, I started drawing. Crayons to pencils to charcoal to fineliners and finally paint, I experimented with all I could.
